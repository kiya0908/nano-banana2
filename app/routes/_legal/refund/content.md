# Refund Policy

Last updated: March 02, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Thank you for using **Nano Banana 2**. This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds are provided for our AI Generator platform and related services. Since our service delivers immediate digital access and expends computational resources upon use, our refund terms strictly reflect this operational reality.

## 2. General Policy

Because Nano Banana 2 is a digital service that processes AI generations in real-time by expending server resources, **all sales are generally final and non-refundable**. We cannot recover the resources used to generate your requests. By completing a purchase, you acknowledge and agree to this no-refund policy.

## 3. Exceptional Circumstances for Refunds

While our standard policy is non-refundable, we may, at our sole discretion, issue refunds or account credits in the following limited circumstances:

1. **Service Unavailability**: If the Service experiences prolonged, widespread downtime that prevents you from entirely utilizing your purchased credits or subscription within a reasonable timeframe.
2. **Duplicate Charges**: If you are charged multiple times for the same transaction due to a billing error on our end.
3. **Major Defect**: If a severe technical error prevents the core AI generation service from returning any result, continuously over a period exceeding 48 hours, and our support team cannot resolve the issue.

Please note that dissatisfaction with the artistic style or subjective quality of the AI-generated results does **not** qualify as a defect and is not eligible for a refund.

## 4. How to Request a Refund

If you believe your situation falls under our exceptional circumstances, please contact us within **7 days** of the original purchase date.

To submit a request, email us at:
**support@nanobanana2pro.space**

Please include the following information in your request:
- The email address associated with your Nano Banana 2 account.
- The transaction ID or receipt number.
- A detailed explanation of why you are requesting a refund, including any relevant screenshots or error logs.

## 5. Processing of Refunds

Once your refund request is received, our support team will review it and notify you of the approval or rejection of your request. This process typically takes up to 5 business days.

If approved, the refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original method of payment. Please allow an additional 5-10 business days for the funds to appear on your statement, depending on your bank or credit card issuer.

## 6. Chargebacks and Disputes

If you file a chargeback or dispute with your payment provider before contacting us to resolve the issue, we reserve the right to immediately suspend or terminate your account without notice. We strongly encourage you to contact our support team first to address any billing concerns.

We may update this Refund Policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
